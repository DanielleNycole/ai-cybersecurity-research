## 🚀 Studying AI in Cybersecurity 🚀

## Introduction

Cybersecurity is changing fast, and one of the biggest shifts happening right now is the integration of **artificial intelligence into both attacks and defenses**.

With the big boom in AI and its rapid growth, I started thinking more seriously about where the industry is headed. It became pretty clear that **AI isn’t just a trend**. It’s actively changing how attackers operate and how defenders have to respond.

## Why This Matters

🚨Attackers are already using AI to:
- Automate phishing campaigns  
- Identify vulnerabilities faster  
- Generate more convincing social engineering attacks  

👉At the same time, companies are rapidly adopting AI tools, sometimes without fully understanding the **security risks they introduce**.

## What This Series Will Cover

In this series, I’ll be breaking down:
- How attackers are using AI  
- How AI is changing traditional attack methods  
- New vulnerabilities introduced by AI  
- How to defend against these threats  

Whenever possible, I’ll include **ethical technical demos** to reinforce what I’m learning.

## Goal

This is part of my journey to grow into a cybersecurity role where I can **analyze emerging threats and contribute to building stronger defenses**.
